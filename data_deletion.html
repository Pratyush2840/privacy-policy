Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to request deletion of your data from our app, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to your@email.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your name and any relevant information about your account or data.
  3. We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the same email address.
